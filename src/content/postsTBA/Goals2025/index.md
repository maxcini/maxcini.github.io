## Discussion 

**What is your dream?**

**What are your goals?** 

These are questions I cannot answer as of writing this. I think this is why myself, along with many other people today, get a sense of "feeling lost" from time to time. 

While your ambitions are likely not exactly the same as mine, I hope by the end of this post we can share a common sense of clarity - understand what it is we want from life, and how to get it. 


















## Context

It shouldn't take a year ending for us to make goals and take steps towards achieving those goals. 

Fortunately, I had a few teachers in school who talked about the importance of setting goals. Unfortunately, I was a smartass and too stupid to listen. 

I thought always having a, "What's next?" mindset would be sufficient in progressing? 

Goal tracking - Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) and Key Success Indicators (KSIs). 

>"If you can't track it, you can't manage it." 

personal testament - trying to hit the green flag in top golf. Albeit this is a small feat with no real prolonged grind for the result, its a simple example that proves the point. You gotta know what your aiming for before you take a swing.

## Notes 

Dave Ramsey (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3U4AUvrlp_0&ab_channel=TheRamseyShowHighlights)
- really big why/reason; having a vision (dreams become a why). Vision going to work = a goal; details to win. Goals have (5) components: 1. specific, 2. measurable, 3. time limit, 4. your goals 5. be in writing. Be in multiple facets of your life (financial, family, physical, etc.)

How to Set Systems, Not Goals (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=y4evLICF8kk&ab_channel=AlexisKingsley)
- SMART goals = incomplete; actionable and timebound disconnected. 
- Last year's ceiling is this year's floor 
- Your dream management system = group of habits strung together to acheive goal. Chunk backwards from dream to develop goals. Dream - best future version of you 
- Top 10 things to make happen, circle one to focus on. Chunk backwards: Year end goal --> Q1 goal --> Monthly outcome goals --> Weekly goals --> daily outcome
- 20% of your time = 80% of your results 
- write goals in the past tense. "I am a millionaire"
